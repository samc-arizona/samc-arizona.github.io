SLO #1: The Rhetorical Situation

In this class I have spent a much bigger part of my writing considering the audience(s) I am writing for that previously. While coming into this class I was pretty comfortable thinking about the rhetorical situation for things I am reading (the intended audience, authors, purpose), I was not used to really digging into the text to find evidence of audience and the rhetorical situation (like I did in Essay 2). Additionally, prior to the class I would think about my audience when I am writing very little, usually only if it was very clear, like I was writing an argument to a few specific people.
