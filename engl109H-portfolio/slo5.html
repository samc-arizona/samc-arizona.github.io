SLO #5: Reflection

This learning objective is one of the ones I think I may have progressed the most in, while at the same time being the one I put the least work into. This is most likely because at the beginning of the semester (and before this class in general), my writing journey and connection to language was not something I had considered/thought/written about at all. Through this class (and especially through the literacy narrative & some of the opening writes) I really began to think about my current and past relationship/experiences with language and writing, and how that affected both how I write and some of the reasons why I might write (outside of specific class assignments).
