SLO #3: Genre Conventions and Language Mechanics

Throughout this class I have definitely grown in my proper use of language mechanics like grammar. This aspect of writing (learning the proper genre conventions and refining my local/minute use of language (grammar/mechanics)) has been one of my favorite to work on/improve in this class. The work I did for the top 20 revised essay 2 especially helped me grow in this aspect. While I did know and was proficient with most grammar before this assignment, I had not (in the past few years) really refreshed my knowledge of specific grammar rules (especially those regarding punctuation), and reading through the textbook for this revision assignment really helped to solidify in my mind the things I already knew, and point out where my knowledge/practice was lacking (mostly regarding use of punctuation like commas or dashes)
